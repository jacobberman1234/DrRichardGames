Finishing games and projects started is a necessity to begin to master game development. In the spirit of this, I’ve decided for this upcoming year to create one game a month all in different genres.


It’s definitely going to be a challenge, but I invite you to participate with me. I am new to hosting things like this, but I feel like participation will not only create more competent game devs, but also build a strong sense of community among us.


The purpose of these game jams is not to have polished games, but at least have complete game mechanics developed, if it’s polished too, well good on you!


In order to increase participation, I’ve created two tiers:


  • Free
  • Paid

Both have their pros, but for the paid tier there’s a prize pool of $100 (hey, it’s something)


Also winners and honorable mentions will have their games played on my YouTube channel


You don't have to commit to either one yet, but the paid entry fee is $5 ON SUBMISSION


Click the button to let me know you're interested in competing!


The submission page will be released the last week of each month!
